To enable customizations, copy the files from the disabled folder into the enabled folder.

Here are the available customization options:


* broesel cross.res - Enables the broesel health cross. Do not use conjunction with other hudplayerhealth files
* prism cross.res - Enables the prism hud health cross next to the health. Do not use conjunction with other hudplayerhealth files
* default cross.res - Enables a cross next to the health value. Do not use conjunction with other hudplayerhealth files
* control point borders.res - Enables black control point borders
* transparent viewmodels - Enables transparent viewmodels

experimental ones:

* scoreboard-minimal.res - Changes the style of the scoreboard to be more minimalistic, suitable for 6v6 or Prolander.
